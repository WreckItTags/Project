RxVendor
PharMachine
(R.V.P.M.)

Revolutionizing Pharmaceutical Convenience with Prescription Reading Vending System

Description

The RxVendor PharMachine aims to revolutionize healthcare accessibility by providing round-the-clock access to medication. Using optical character recognition, it reads and validates handwritten prescriptions, ensuring patients receive the correct drugs. Placed strategically, it will optimize patient care, reduce wait times, and enhance the distribution of medications. The objective is to streamline the healthcare system, providing an easy-to-use, efficient, and accessible solution for prescription medicine retrieval. By deploying this automated system, we aim to make pharmacy lines shorter while ensuring patient data security and confidentiality.

FEATURES

DISPLAY PANEL (ITEM SELECTOR)

This is the user interface that allows customers to select the products that they want to purchase. It consists of a series of buttons that correspond to the different drugs available in the machine. The display panel may also include other information, such as the price of each item, nutritional information, and advertising.

PAYMENT FUNCTION

It gives change by incorporating a combination of sensors, a cash acceptor, a coin dispenser, and a microcontroller.

PRESCRIPTION SCANNER

This allows patients to quickly and easily pick up their prescriptions without having to wait in line at a pharmacy. To use the prescription scanner, patients would simply input their receipt or prescription from their doctor to be scanned by the machine. The vending machine would then identify the prescription based from a certain database of pharmacist codes and dispense the medication after confirmation and full payment.

DIRECT BENEFICIARIES

PATIENTS

This will assist patients by giving them easy access to necessary medications, improving their overall experience with healthcare.

PHARMACISTS

Pharmacists will benefit from this project through increased efficiency in the fulfillment of prescriptions, freeing up more time for patient care, which enables them to focus on providing in-depth medication counseling, addressing complex medication-related issues, and lowering the chance of medication errors, thereby improving the healthcare services they provide.

HEALTHCARE FACILITIES

Healthcare facilities will benefit from this project by achieving cost savings through automated medication dispensing and reduced labor requirements. The system optimizes inventory management, minimizing medication waste and enabling healthcare facilities to allocate resources more efficiently, ultimately enhancing operational efficiency and cost-effectiveness.
