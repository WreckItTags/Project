COMPONENTS

KEYPAD

The keypad is essential for smooth system interaction, allowing users to input product codes, specify quantities, and authenticate for secure access. It enhances vending machine efficiency by enabling fast and accurate transactions.

LCD DISPLAY

The screen provides a user-friendly display of medical products with details and prices, improving browsing and decision-making. It ensures transparency by showing transaction details and supports essential notifications like user prompts and confirmations, enhancing communication between the vending machine and users.

CAMERA MODULE LENS

The Camera Module Lens is vital in our medical vending machine project, enhancing security, monitoring, and user interaction. It is integral to the machine's surveillance system, offering real-time visual monitoring of the vending area for safety. By promoting security, enabling user authentication, and supporting inventory management, the Camera Module Lens significantly boosts the functionality of our medical vending machine.

COIN/BILL CHANGER

The Coin/Bill Changer is pivotal for smooth transactions, accepting and dispensing coins and bills, ensuring user convenience. By offering a reliable payment mechanism and accommodating various denominations, it enhances the vending machine's accessibility and user-friendliness, catering to individuals without exact change.

COIN AND BILL STORAGE

The Coin and Bill Storage unit is used to promote financial security and integrity. It facilitates accurate accounting of the funds collected, helping to prevent theft and unauthorized access. Additionally, the secure storage of coins and bills ensures a reliable and continuous availability of change for users, promoting a smooth and uninterrupted vending process.

POWER SUPPLY

Its primary role involves converting incoming electrical power to the required voltage levels essential for the optimal functioning of the vending machine. The Power Supply ensures the continuous and dependable operation of electronic elements such as the display screen, keypad, and currency handling mechanisms. By providing essential power, this component contributes to the overall stability and functionality of the vending machine, enabling seamless transactions and ensuring users can access medical supplies without interruptions.

ARDUINO MEGA

The Arduino Mega is responsible for processing input from user interfaces such as the keypad and coin/bill changer, managing data from sensors, and controlling the output to components like the LCD display and dispensing mechanisms. Its programmability and versatility make it a key contributor to the project's success, allowing for the implementation of complex algorithms, user authentication, transaction processing, and communication with other components. The Arduino Mega enhances the adaptability and efficiency of our medical vending machine by serving as a robust and programmable control system that orchestrates seamless interactions between the user and the machine's functionalities.

SERVO MOTOR

The Servo Motor is a critical component that facilitates controlled and precise movements within the system. This motor plays a crucial role in the dispensing mechanism, ensuring accurate and reliable delivery of medical supplies to users. The Servo Motor is responsible for regulating the opening and closing of the dispensing compartments, allowing the user to access the selected products. Its precise movement capabilities contribute to the overall efficiency of the vending machine by ensuring that the correct items are dispensed in the right quantities. The Servo Motor is programmable, allowing for customization based on specific product sizes and quantities, enhancing the flexibility and adaptability of the vending machine.

BREADBOARD

The Breadboard serves as a versatile and temporary platform for prototyping and testing electronic circuits. This component contributes to the project by providing a convenient and adaptable space for assembling and experimenting with various electrical components before their integration into the vending machine's final design. The Breadboard facilitates the development and refinement of circuitry involving components such as sensors, buttons, and LEDs, allowing for systematic testing and troubleshooting. Its modular design allows quick changes and adjustments without the need for soldering, making the prototyping phase more efficient and flexible.

JUMPER RIBBON CABLE

This flat ribbon cable with attached connectors enables the seamless transmission of signals and power between different modules, enhancing the overall wiring efficiency of the vending machine. The Jumper Ribbon Cable is particularly valuable in scenarios where space constraints or intricate circuit layouts require a compact and neat wiring solution. Its flexibility allows for easy routing and bending, adapting to the spatial constraints within the vending machine.

ACRYLIC SHEET

This component contributes significantly to the project by providing a clear and durable surface for informational displays, ensuring users can easily view product information, pricing, and transaction details. Additionally, the acrylic sheet serves a protective function by enclosing the internal components, safeguarding them from external elements while allowing visibility for monitoring and maintenance purposes.

STEEL COMPRESSION SPRING

This spring plays a crucial role in providing controlled and reliable force for the dispensing process, ensuring that medical products are dispensed accurately and consistently. By utilizing alloy steel, this compression spring offers durability and resilience, with the ability to withstand repetitive compressions without losing its effectiveness.

LED STRIP LIGHT PLASTIC AND ELECTRONIC

Comprising both plastic housing and electronic components, serves a dual-purpose, contributing to both functionality and user experience. This component is strategically integrated to illuminate the interior of the vending machine, allowing users to clearly see and assess the available medical products. The LED strip light enhances visibility, aiding users in navigating the machine's interface and making informed selections. The plastic housing ensures durability and protection for the electronic elements, ensuring the longevity of the lighting system.
